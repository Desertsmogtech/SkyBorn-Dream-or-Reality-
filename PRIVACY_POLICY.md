# Privacy Policy (SkyBorn-Dream-or-Reality)

This repository does not intentionally collect, store, or process personal data.

Operator identity, personal history, lineage, rituals, or any operator-only content:
- MUST NOT be committed to this repository
- MUST NOT be shared via issues, pull requests, or comments
- MUST remain under the operator's direct control, offline or in a separate, private medium

If you choose to share any personal information in this repository, you do so at your own discretion and risk.
